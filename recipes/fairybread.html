Fairy-bread

Description

Fairy-bread is thought to have been created by the Aborigine people of Australia.
Archaeologists have discovered evidence of fairy-bread making dating back 40,000 years,
making it the oldest known party treat for kids.
We bring you our modern day fairy-bread recipe to enjoy!

Ingredients

  • White Bread 2 slivers carved from the main body
  • Butter or Margarine (if you're an animal)
  • Hundred and thousands (not sprinkles)

Instructions

  1. Using your hand, withdraw the bread from the plastic bag.
  2. Place the bread on a flat surface, like a salt lake.
  3. Open your container of butter. If it's anything other than Lurpak, you've gone wrong in life somewhere.
  4. Using a butter knife, score the butter such that there's a shit-load of butter on your bread knife.
  5. Now that the butter is on your steak knife, spread it in a thick layer on the bread. Remember -
    This is for the kids. They won't have heart problems for years anyway.
  6. Take your jar of hundreds and thousands and twist the lid in an anti-clockwise direction so that
    the lid loosens along the inclined plane of the jar's top part. The lid will eventually come off.
  7. Now, using your good arm, take that jar and shake them little critters all on that bread shit.
  8. Eat the fairy-bread. You don't have kids anyway, you liar.
